As a student of Mathematics and Physics I feel the need to explain the things I study in order to make sure that I really understand them. I usually ask myself questions and try to answer them in the simplest way possible. It is then when I realize the aspects in which I need to put more emphasis on throughout the study. Bearing this in mind and realizing that what I was doing could actually be of interest to somebody else I decided to start this blog.

All work shown here is thus the result of my lecture notes, book reading and self-study. It is therefore very likely to encounter errors when reading it. I would really appreciate that the reader made me aware of them either by leaving a comment or by sending an email.

Topics covered will be varied, but the idea is to write about core concepts in order to make them as clear as possible. I will try not to leave any aspect untouched and always try to make my posts self-contained.
